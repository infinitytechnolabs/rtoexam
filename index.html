Privacy Policy for RTO Exam Application

Last Updated: 29th September, 2024

At Infinity Technolabs, we value the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting any personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard information related to our RTO Exam application, which provides users with a list of questions, traffic signs, mock exams, and multi-language support (English, Hindi, and Gujarati). Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your privacy.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our application does not require any form of login, nor do we collect, store, or access any personal data. We do not ask for personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying details.

2. No Account Required

Our application does not require users to create accounts or log in to access the features. You can use all features, including viewing questions, traffic signs, taking mock exams, and using multi-language support, without providing any personal information.

3. Features of the App

The key features of our app include:

  • List of Questions: Users can view various practice questions related to the RTO exam.
  • Traffic Signs: Information and learning materials regarding traffic signs are provided.
  • Mock Exam: Users can take mock exams to prepare for the actual RTO test.
  • Multi-Language Support: The app supports English, Hindi, and Gujarati languages, and users can switch between these languages.

4. No Data Access or Sharing

We do not collect or access any data from users, and thus we do not share any information with third parties. Since we do not track or store your activities or personal details, there is no risk of data being shared or sold.

5. Third-Party Services

Our application does not use any third-party services that may collect or process personal data. We have ensured that no external analytics tools or advertising networks are integrated into the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application is not designed to collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of unauthorized access or use of children's information.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
